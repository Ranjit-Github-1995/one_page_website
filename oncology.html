Oncology Massage

Here at Immersive Massage Company, we specialise in oncology massage for people living with cancer. Recognised as a valuable complementary therapy by the NHS, studies have shown that a relaxation light touch massage can help in the relief of pain, anxiety and fatigue, and can also improve quality of life. Combined with the relaxing ambience and soothing 3D visuals we provide, your treatment will leave you with a deep feeling of serenity and sense of wellbeing.

How an Oncology Massage Works

There are two main mechanisms at work which affect the patient. Massage increases blood-flow bringing nutrients to the muscles, while also removing toxins via the lymphatic system. This establishes an ideal environment for healthy cells to live in. The second mechanism is stimulation of the nervous system through touch. This has a direct effect on the brain and central nervous system, raising levels of calm and wellbeing, consistent with diminished pain and anxiety. In-turn, lower stress levels improve our client’s quality of sleep.

Our Most Common Question: Can Having a Massage Spread Cancer?

Cancer is the uncontrolled growth of cells in the body. This fault arises from the fact such cancerous cells are no longer able to read their DNA correctly, continuing to divide when they should have stopped or failing to recognise the programmed self-destruct mechanism of normal gene expression.

There is a widely held belief that massage can break-off cancerous cells from a tumour, facilitating spread, typically through the lymphatic system.However, there is no clinical evidence that massage does cause such secondary cancers. Indeed, the American National Cancer Institute concludes there is no data to support this and recognises that massage has a positive effect for those living with cancer.

Cancers generally form when individual cells become irritated due to elevated toxicity in or inflammation of their natural environment. Here at Immersive Massage Company, we only ever use cold pressed organic oils in order not to add any irritants into your body.
